Our Story

Founded on Excellence

Nu Tech Auto Clinic was established in 2007 with a simple mission: to bring German automotive engineering standards to South Africa. What started as a small workshop has grown into Durban's most trusted automotive service center.

Our journey began when founder Michael Roux, a certified VW-Audi technician, returned from Germany with advanced diagnostic equipment and a passion for precision engineering. Today, we're proud to be RMI approved and serve over 2,000 satisfied customers annually.
